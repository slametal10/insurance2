Lockton Companies: Global Insurance Brokerage Leader

Company Snapshot: Founded 1966 • HQ: Kansas City, MO • $3.1B revenue (2023) • 10,750+ employees • 130+ offices • 140+ countries served • World's largest privately held insurance broker

Lockton Companies has established itself as the world's largest privately held insurance brokerage firm, offering comprehensive risk management, insurance, and employee benefits solutions to clients across diverse industries. With its unique client-first approach and entrepreneurial culture, Lockton has maintained consistent organic growth for over five decades while remaining family-owned.

Company History and Milestones

1966: Humble Beginnings

Jack Lockton founded the company at age 24 in Kansas City, Missouri, shortly after graduating from the University of Missouri-Kansas City with an economics degree. The startup began with just a handful of employees serving local businesses.

1970s: Establishing Foundations

Lockton developed specialized expertise in sports and entertainment insurance, building relationships with professional sports teams that remain clients today. The firm expanded throughout the Midwest region.

1980s-1990s: Strategic Growth

The company expanded nationally through organic growth and strategic acquisitions, establishing offices in major U.S. markets. Lockton developed its distinctive "Uncommonly Independent" philosophy during this period.

2000s: Global Expansion

Following Jack Lockton's passing in 2004, his brother David Lockton led international expansion into Europe, Asia, and Latin America. The company surpassed $1 billion in revenue in 2008.

2010s-Present: Market Leadership

Lockton solidified its position as the world's largest private broker, reaching $3.1 billion in revenue by 2023. The company continues expanding its service offerings and geographic footprint while maintaining private ownership.
