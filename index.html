Westfield Insurance: Protecting What Matters Since 1848

Key Facts: Founded in 1848 as Ohio Farmers Insurance, Westfield has grown into a multi-line insurer with over $2.4 billion in annual premiums, serving customers through 1,000+ independent agents nationwide while maintaining strong financial ratings.

Company History and Evolution

Westfield Insurance traces its roots to 1848 when a group of Ohio farmers banded together to form Ohio Farmers Insurance Company, creating a mutual protection system for their properties. This agricultural beginning shaped the company's community-focused values that persist today.

1848 - Founded as Ohio Farmers Insurance Company
Early 1900s - Expands beyond agricultural insurance
1980s - Forms Westfield Group as parent company
2000s - Expands nationally through independent agents
2025 - Named Best Small Business Insurance by Forbes
